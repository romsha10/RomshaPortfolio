I am a Data Scientist and MSc candidate at the University of Glasgow, specialising in Machine Learning, Fair Ranking, and ESG Analytics. My work sits at the intersection of rigorous technical research and real-world impact - I build systems that don't just predict well, but predict fairly.

I've worked across healthcare analytics, financial fraud detection, ESG intelligence, and government data - building end-to-end pipelines, deploying live tools, and presenting findings to non-technical stakeholders. I hold a Gold badge in both SQL and Python on HackerRank, and certifications from Google, Michigan, and Microsoft.

I'm based in Glasgow, available immediately on a Graduate Route Visa, with full right to work across the UK.
